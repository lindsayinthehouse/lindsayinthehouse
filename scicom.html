SciCom & Press

I think sharing my research and excitement about space with the public is just as important as doing the research itself. Below you'll find a portfolio of my science communication work. I began with short and long-form videos and podcasts for various outlets, including NPR, CBS, NBC, Adler Planetarium, and NASA. In addition, written pieces for SCIENCE and Astronomy Magazine. I am always looking for new interesting ways to communicate science, so if you'd like to collaborate, please reach out at lindsay.r.house@gmail.com.
